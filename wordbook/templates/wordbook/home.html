English is not my native language. I understand how hard it can be for anyone to learn new languages. This is why I created the Word2Mem. Word2Mem is a personal vocabulary builder, designed for anyone who is interested in improving their vocabulary.

With Word2Mem, you can:

  • Create your personal account to start your learning journey
  • Create multiple vocabulary collections
  • Import your own vocabularies in CSV or Excel format
  • Learn new vocabularies and review old ones with interactive Flashcard learning sessions
  • Review your past performance
  • More features coming soon!
